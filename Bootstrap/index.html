Thinking about how to brew rich coffee ?

COLD BREW

Cold brew coffee originated in Japan, where it has been a traditional method of coffee brewing for centuries also known as Kyoto-style / Dutch coffee in East Asia.

  
FLASH BREW

Flash brewing is a Japanese style of cold coffee brewing. It is similar to drip coffee.

MOKA

coffee brewed with a moka pot, a stovetop coffee maker which produces coffee by passing hot water pressurized by steam through ground coffee at a lower pressure than an espresso maker.

  
MANILO

A Manilo coffee consists of a regular espresso shot and less than 100ml of silky milk. Popularised due to its strength and taste, without a lot of milk. Similar to a half flat white, but slightly smaller.
